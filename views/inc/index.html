Is the exciting new social network that takes 10 photos and turns them into an animation that can tilt in your hand.

Imagine if photos could react to your hand movements?

That is the question we asked ourselves, and Tilt is the result. It's a completely new visual medium, and a whole new way to express your creativity.

Simply tilt your phone left or right to animate your photos

Tilt a simple and effective way to create moments that feel alive. By tilting your phone left or right, the image reacts to your hand movements so you feel like you are controlling it - that you are making the pictures “move”. A tilt photo feels more like a “window” than a photo. A window into that moment in time, one that you can relive over and over again. Kind of like “living pictures”.

Make breathtaking panoramas, photo collages or funny animations.

It is the perfect tool when 1 photo is just not enough. When you want to share a moment, now you can - from 10 different angles. Capture those little details that would otherwise be missed and forgotten forever.

Point, shoot, animate. It’s easy to make tilt photos

Making a tilt photo animation is easy. With our camera, you can take photos or select photos from your photo albums. There are small thumbnails underneath the camera view that show you the order of your sequence. Preview the sequence as a Tilt animation, and add one of our beautiful photo filters if you like. Then add a caption then post it to your followers.

Create a profile. Follow your friends. Explore tilt photos!

It’s fun to make Tilt animations, but it’s also lots of fun seeing what other people create as well. In the Explore tab, you can see what’s new from around the world. See how other people are using Tilt and be inspired by their creativity!

Share and view your photos on the web, at tilt.photos

You can view your tilt animation on a webpage, and share the link on Facebook and Twitter for your friends to see. You can also embed the tilt photo on your own blog or website with our custom embed code.
